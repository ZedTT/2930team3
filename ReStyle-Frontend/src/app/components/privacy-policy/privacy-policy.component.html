Privacy Policy
ReStyle is a web/mobile application used to assist users in finding clothing items they want to trade with other users.

We collect information you provide us directly:
  • Your username, password and email address when you create an account following login
  • Any additional information you provide when you edit your profile such as city, postal code, phone number
  • Your profile and item images you provide, such as the photos that are added for items for trading and the profile picture you upload when you edit your profile
  • User content such as the users involved in trading
